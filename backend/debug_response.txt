## Photosynthesis MCQs

1. What is the main purpose of photosynthesis?
A) To produce energy
B) To convert sunlight into food
C) To release oxygen as a byproduct
D) To clean the air
Answer: B) To convert sunlight into food

2. Which of these gases is produced during photosynthesis? 
A) Oxygen
B) Carbon dioxide
C) Methane
D) Nitrogen
Answer: A) Oxygen
